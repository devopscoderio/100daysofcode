A Rat’s Paradise

Luton is bustling with opportunities if you know where to look. The streets are alive with dropped snacks, and the back alleys behind the eateries are pure goldmines. The people here are generous – always leaving behind half a sandwich or a chip. And the warmth of the underground spaces? Perfect for a cozy hideout. This place is my empire.

Pigeon Perspective

From the rooftops of Luton, life looks busy and full of opportunity. There’s always a warm air vent to perch by and plenty of crumbs to peck at in the town square. The humans here are noisy but entertaining, and the open skies mean plenty of room for a quick getaway when needed. Luton is perfect for a city-savvy pigeon like me.
